About the game

Homeland Defense Simulator is a tower defense game made with GDevelop, assets made with Piskel and existing assets fetched from Kenney. (See credits here) The game has three maps with differing difficulties, Grasslands being the easiest and Sky being the hardest.

Wave progression

Wave progression system works simply killing all of the enemies and not letting your castle that you are defending die. Each wave passing gains you money depending on your castle's health. Also as you get far from the waves, enemy mutation will occur, either having heighten speed or stronger health. Boss would spawn at the last wave and its difficulty in killing it depends on what map.
