How to play

Controls

This game is primary point-and-click, easily to work with. Left Mouse Button - Interact Right Mouse Button - Sell tower

Objectives

Waves and Maps vary on how much enemies are spawned per wave. progress and get to the next wave, you need to kill all of the enemies, once at the last wave, you need to kill the boss. Just don't let castle die from the enemies,
