Keyboard Controls

  • W,A,S,D keys — Move around your character
  • J key — Shoot fireballs to attack the dragons
  • Spacebar — Jump

How to Play

Objectives

Go deeper into the dungeon, burn all the dragons in the way, and collect their souls.

Tips for Survival

  • Use fireballs to destroy the dragon attacks and dodge if you miss.
  • Watchout for the lava pits, and dont touch the dragons, they could burn you.
  • Jump on top of the flying dragons to kill them.
