Characters

Little Mario

Little Mario is the son of Mario. He is on a mission to complete the same journey as his dad did. He will be riding his go kart, allowing him to notice everything quickly.

Goomba

Goomba is on a mission to not let Little Mario complete the course. He tries to block Little Mario’s way, by crawling back and forth on the floating platforms.

Green Shell

Green Shell is on a vengeance run. For the past couple decades, his family members have been owned by Little Mario’s dad. Green Shell wants his family to be honored, so it is ready to do whatever it takes to stop Little Mario.

Toad

Toad was the longtime companion for Mario. They both completed the course together. Toad is no longer living, but he is still trying to help Little Mario. His head will gift Little Mario another life, helping to protect him.
