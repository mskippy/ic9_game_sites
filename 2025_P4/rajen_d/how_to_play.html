How To Play

  • The purpose of the game is to complete all 3 levels with as many points as possible.
  • Each level gets harder than the previous one.
  • You start each level with 3 lives, but you can collect more throughout the game.
  • Enemies:
  • Goomba: Moves left to right. Jump on its head to kill it and get an extra jump. Running into it costs 1 life. It gets faster each level.
  • Green Shell: Moves faster than the Goomba. Jump on it to destroy it. Colliding with it costs 1 life. It becomes faster each level.
  • Pineapple: Spawns from green tubes and kills you instantly. Cannot be destroyed.
  • Green Shell Shooter: Spawns shells from above that fall downwards. Only appears in Level 3.
  • Power Ups and Power Downs:
  • Coin Box: Appears in all levels. Hitting it from below gives points depending on the level.
  • Pizza: Appears in all levels. Moves either in a circular motion or left to right. Gives points on collision.
  • Soda Can: Appears in all levels. Moves in a circular motion. Colliding with it subtracts points.
  • Toad: Stays still in each level. Gives 1 extra heart on collision.
