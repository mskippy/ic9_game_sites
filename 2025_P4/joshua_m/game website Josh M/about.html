Lore

Jamal is a father of two kids who has a poker addiction. One day he lost all his money playing poker and his wife then left him taking his kids and leaving him with nothing. Sad and depressed he headed over to the local volcano to end his life but after jumping he wanted a second chance at life and now, he must escape to see his daughters again.

Creator

Lava Jump was created by Joshua Mullins, a grade 9 student at sutherland secondary, using Gdevelop's software.
