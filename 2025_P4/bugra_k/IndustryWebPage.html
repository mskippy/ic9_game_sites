Industry Run is a fast-paced pixel-style game made in GDevelop. Run through industrial zones, avoid hazards, kill enemies, and explore mechanical environments.
GAME FEATURES
Industrial pixel environments
Smooth, responsive movement
Traps, machinery, and hazards
Upgradeable tools and items
Progressive difficulty
GAME LORE
In a industry that uses cyborgs, you want to escape. Broken conveyor belts, rusted pipes, and rogue machinery create a dangerous space that only the bold will explore. Hidden deep within the collapsed factories are strange glowing cores that hint at forgotten technology.

You play as a lone runner want to escape. Escaping the shifting machinery that roams the corridors. Every area reveals more fragments of the story.
MECHANICS
- Pixel-accurate collision detection
- Timed obstacles and pressure traps
- Upgradeable running speed, jump height, and tool durability
- Increasing hazard complexity the farther you go
