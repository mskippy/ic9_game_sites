How to Play

The controls are are W and A to move and Space to jump. Through out the game you collect coins, the plane yellow ones are worth 1, the yellow and silver ones worth 50 and the 100 dollar bills worth 100. Runing into enemys will make you lose a hear, lose all four and you will have to restart the level.

Tips and Tricks

Try mastering the distance George can jump, if you miss a jump, you could lose a lot of progress. Knowing the movements of enemies can help you avoid them. Not having fun is the leading cause of unhappiness. If you are having trouble at any point, try a different approach.
