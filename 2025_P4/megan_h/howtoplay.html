How to Play

Learn the basics and jump into the action

Getting Started

Welcome to My Game! Your goal is to explore the world, overcome challenges, and survive as long as possible. Use your skills, timing, and strategy to win.

Controls

  • W / A / S / D – Move
  • Space – Jump

Objective

Complete levels, avoid enemies, and collect power-ups. The further you progress, the harder the game becomes.

Combat

Enemies will appear as you explore. Attack, dodge, and use abilities wisely to survive encounters.

Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid damage
  • Save power-ups for tough moments
  • Learn enemy patterns
