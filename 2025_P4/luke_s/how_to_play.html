How to Play

Controls

Use ← → ↑ ↓ to move your character. Press Space to jump.

Goal

You play as a spaceman.

Collect all the jewels while avoiding obstacles to reach the end of the level.

Watch out for the Enemies.

Avoid the Traps.

Saw Blade
Fan
Fire
Spikes

Tips

  • Time your jumps carefully.
  • Look for hidden paths.
  • Try not to run out of lives.
