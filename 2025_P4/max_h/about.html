Game Story

Jim is a ,a WW2 veteran who has severe Ptsd from the war, and s Bob was Jim's best friend who he swore to protect, but unfortunatley Bob was killed in action. And the game is Jim's nightmare and it is your duty to escape his nightmares before he goes mad. Survive at all costs...

How to Play

  • Movement keys: W to jump, and S and A to move left and right or Arrow keys and left click to shoot
  • Movement keys: I to jump, and J and L to move left and right or Arrow keys
  • Deep blue uses I,J,L and light blue uses W,A,D and left click.
