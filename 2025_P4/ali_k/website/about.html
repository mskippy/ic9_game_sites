The Idea Behind the Game

This game is inspired by classic comic-book art, using bold outlines, vibrant colours, and dramatic shadows to capture the superhero feel. The mission is simple: defend Chicago from incoming threats while swinging through the city as Spider-Man.

Art Style

The design follows a strong colour palette featuring hero red (#ff5722), hero blue (#0091ea), black, and white. Every element—from buttons to panels—keeps the dramatic comic-book vibe.
