Spider-Man

The city's web-slinging protector. Agile, brave, and always ready to save Chicago.He is known for being clumsy and making mistakes while trying to help others. Even though he is not very good at being a superhero, he always tries his best.

Green Goblin

A chaotic villain flying around causing havoc. Expect fast attacks and unpredictable movement.He enjoys terrorizing people, blowing things up, and watching the city burn.

Spoder-man

Spoder-man is a hero trying to save the city of Chicago. He is on a mission to keep the city safe from super villians. He will be jumping from building to building in his red and blue suit.

Goblin Green

Goblin Green is on a mission to rob banks and terrorize the city of Chicago. He tries to be evil and be a super vilian. He flys on a hoverboad and he has orange bombs he throws at people.
