Story / How to Play

Story

You are a minimum-wage factory worker stuck on the late shift inside a decaying radioactive facility. After a containment breach triggers an explosion, the factory begins collapsing, trapping you inside. The radiation awakens long-buried workers who were “lost” in old waste containers, and now they roam the halls as glowing, unstable mutants. Your only goal: find a way out before the entire factory melts down.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys to move through the factory.
  • Avoid radioactive pools, hazards, and patrolling zombies.
  • Collect hammers to gain checkpoints.
  • Monitor your death count — if it reaches 10, you’re done.
