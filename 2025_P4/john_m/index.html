Overview

The Legends of Lelda is a platform game inspired by The Legends of Zelda. The goal of the game is to go to the end of the levels without dying and go to difficult terrain to save Lelda, as she got captured by evil goblins. The main character, Zink, battles through strong enemies and as well as traps when going past all the levels. Level one begins in a peaceful plain where Zink learns how to jump on the goblins and to jump over pits of spikes. Level two takes place in an ice-cold tundra, filled with spikes and lots of goblins. Level three leads into a fiery volcano with pits of lava everywhere. The final level is the boss's room. It has doubled the number of goblins at all levels and is in a compact space to keep Lelda captured. .
