How to Play

Controls

  • Left / Right Arrow Keys — Move
  • Space / Up Arrow Key — Jump
  • Right Click Mouse — Attack
  • E — Interact with characters

Game Goal

Your goal as the princess is to safely go through guards, and obstacles as to escape the castle and uncover that the only choice is to defeat the King. Reach the end of each level to progress through the story.

Tips

  • Watch enemy patterns before attacking.
