How to Play

Controls

  • Start: Click on start to begin the game otherwise it wont begin.
  • Shop: Click and place defenders from the shop.
  • Deletion: Click on defenders to delete them.

Goal

The goal of Block TD is to strategically plan, place, and manage different troops to defend your base from continuous waves of invading blocks, using smart tactics, upgrades, and careful resource management to survive and win each level.

Tips

  • Have good placement.
  • Use money well.
  • Have a strategy.
