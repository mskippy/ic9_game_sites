Story

Your mission

When the sun fades, you rise

Deep in the silent dark of space, our sun is about to be destroyed. As a professional person that works in space, you are the only one who can fix it. You will explore colorful cosmic fields, collect scattered stars, and combine them to build a brand new sun.

Simple yet beautiful: every element is designed to feel clean, friendly, and inviting, so anyone can pick up the game and instantly feel at home in space.

Core loop

Collect, craft, ignite

Travel through star fields, grab as many stars as you can, and merge them into a glowing, golden new sun.

Atmosphere

Space vibes that match

Vibrant rockets and stars sit perfectly on a dark space background, creating a calm but exciting mood.

Visual style

Friendly and welcoming

Rounded shapes, soft shadows, and bright colors make the game feel approachable for all ages.

Design mood

Baked fresh at golden hour

The warm yellow and pink tones mimic golden‑hour light, while deep blues and teals keep everything grounded in space.

Style guide

Rocket colors & space mood
