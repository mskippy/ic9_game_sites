Main Character – Reddy

Reddy is sure there’s a sparkly treasure chest waiting for him in the deep ocean. Everyone else says it’s just a story, but Reddy never stops dreaming! With your help, he’ll swim, snack, and grow until he’s finally ready to open the magical chest.
