How to Play

Learn how to survive, grow, and explore the deep!

Game Controls

  • Main Control: Click and hold the left mouse button to grab the fish.
  • Movement: Drag your mouse to move the fish around the screen.
  • Release: Let go of the mouse button to stop moving.

Goal

Eat smaller creatures to grow stronger. As you grow, you can explore deeper areas, unlock new abilities, and survive longer in the dangerous ocean. But be careful— the deep is filled with threats!

Dangers

  • Bigger Fish: Stay away from large predators that can eat you instantly.
  • Nets: Avoid floating nets—once you're caught, you can't escape!
  • Seaweed: Thick patches of seaweed can slow you down and trap you.

Tips

  • Move smoothly—quick jerks can drag you into danger.
  • Eat small creatures first to grow safely before exploring deeper areas.
  • Watch for nets and seaweed hiding near the ocean floor.
